Tomás

Early Adopter

Tomás has been a Mozilla contributor for years and belongs to Mozilla Reps. He knows the ins and outs of the organization and has landed code on several projects including Firefox, Lightbeam and Webmaker. While he is usually the first to try out new technologies, he is keen to protect his ideals and cares a lot about privacy, Net Neutrality and open access. His day job is as a college teacher so Tomás is always looking for ways to get his students excited about the web.
