Informal Educator

Deepa

Deepa enjoys teaching people around her web skills because she remembers when learning the web seemed impossible. Many of her students are young, but she also holds some nighttime classes for adults at a community building. Her students go on to sell things they make online, study computer science at university, or use tech to create portfolios and get jobs. She needs a teaching infrastructure that allows her students to keep learning even when they aren't "in class" together.
